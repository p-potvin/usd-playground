# Roadmap: USD Digital Twin project

## Milestone 1: Gaussian Splat Pipeline (current)

Goal: Successfully reconstruct a 3D scene from video and export to PLY.

## Milestone 2: Native OpenUSD Assets

Goal: Convert PLY to OpenUSD using the 26.03 schema and establish an asset library.

## Milestone 3: Real-time Simulation & Robotics

Goal: Ingest USD assets into Isaac Sim and validate physics/camera navigation.

## Milestone 4: Cosmos AI Enhancement

Goal: Integrate Cosmos models for automated annotation and photorealistic domain transfer.
